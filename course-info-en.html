Professional Semi-Permanent Makeup Course

Bellani's semi-permanent makeup course
is designed based on 15 years of hands-on treatment experience,
structured for systematic learning from basics to practice.

Not just a follow-along class, but one centered on
design principles and skin understanding,
with the goal of practical training immediately applicable in the field.

Curriculum

01. Semi-Permanent Theory Basics

  • Basic concepts of semi-permanent makeup
  • Skin type characteristics and color understanding
  • Pigment types and color correction principles
  • Hygiene management and precautions

02. Design & Pattern Understanding

  • Facial proportions and eyebrow bone structure analysis
  • Design basics tailored to client face shapes
  • How to achieve left-right balance
  • Pattern practice and design revision methods

03. Machine & Tool Usage

  • Understanding semi-permanent machine structure
  • Needle types and usage
  • Pressure control and depth management
  • Techniques for lighter hand pressure

04. Practical Training

  • Practice on artificial skin
  • Line expression practice
  • Gradation and color expression
  • Repeated treatment flow practice

Who Is This For

  • Those considering starting semi-permanent makeup
  • Those with basic skills but lacking practical experience
  • Those wanting to deepen design and longevity understanding
