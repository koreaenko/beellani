Philosophy

With 15 years of hands-on experience,
Bellani values consistent quality over quick results,
and personalized standards over trends.

We don't apply the same design to every client.
Considering eye shape, facial proportions, muscle movement, and daily care habits,
we aim for natural results that last, not just for today but over time.

What 15 years of facing countless faces has taught us:
"Precise judgment lasts longer than excessive technique."
Bellani is a studio that has upheld this standard.

Hygiene Management

Over many years in the field,
we've come to realize that hygiene is not optional, but a fundamental prerequisite for results.

Bellani manages all treatment tools
with a per-client individual management principle.
Products and tools that come in direct contact with the skin
are thoroughly managed according to established hygiene standards.

Our treatment spaces are always maintained with cleaning and sterilization as the standard,
and our hygiene processes are transparently communicated so clients can feel confident.

15 years of experience was not just about technique,
but about knowing which standards must be upheld.
